* 作者 / Author: 紫心醉梦

### Overview

A comprehensive enhancement mod that improves resource acquisition efficiency, creature stat caps, and Nether Stone enhancements.Feature List

#### 1. More Base Resources

* Battle resource acquisition x2
* Chest loot quantity x3
* God favor acquisition x10

#### 2. More Medals

When picking up a medal, receive additional medals of the same type:

* Base additional quantity: 4
* Bonus based on the highest level creature in your party (+1 per 20 levels)
* Additional quantity cap: 25 (maximum total of 30)

#### 3. More Materials

When picking up materials, receive 4 additional materials of the same type.

#### 4. Talisman Level Cap x2

The maximum upgrade level for all talismans is doubled.

Note: Level 1 talismans are unaffected (only applies to talismans with a maximum level greater than 1).

#### 5. Luck Cap Increase

The luck cap is increased from 100% to 500%.

#### 6. Nether Stone Enhancement

* Special affix cap: 3 → 5
* Dynamic affix probability bonus: Automatically increases affix trigger probability based on the highest level creature in your party. Bonus = Highest Level / 10 (capped at 100%)

#### 7. Creature Maximum Stat Multiplier x50

The maximum multiplier for creature base stats (Attack, Defense, Health, etc.) is increased from the original 5 to 50.

#### 8. Fusion Restriction Removed

Removes the filtering restriction on "already used creatures" in the fusion system, allowing the same creature to be used repeatedly in fusions. (When fusing again, the additional trait will be replaced by the new one.)

#### 9\. Creature Maximum Level Doubled

#### 10\. Realm Nether Boss Drop Rate Increased

#### 11\. Realm Item Bonus Increased

* Base 3x bonus, with an additional 1x bonus for every 40 levels of the highest level creature

#### 12\. Gem Charge Greatly Increased

* Only applies to newly generated gems

#### 13\. Master of All and Noble Perk Enhanced

* Master of All maximum level changed to 5, Noble effect doubled

### Important Notes

This mod significantly alters the game's numerical balance.

* Signatures may break after game updates. Please check for author updates.
* Some features rely on AOB signature scanning. If a scan fails, the corresponding feature will be automatically skipped. Check 'mod_loader.log' to verify which features are active.

### 简介

综合增强 Mod，提升资源获取效率、生物属性上限与下界石增强。



### **功能列表：**

#### 1. 更多基础资源

* 战斗资源获取量x2
* 宝箱战利品数量 x3
* 神明恩惠获取量 x10

#### 2. 更多勋章

拾取勋章时，额外获得同类勋章：

* 基础额外数量：4 个
* 根据队伍中生物的最高等级追加（每20 级 +1 个），额外数量上限：25 个（即最多总计 30 个）

#### 3. 更多材料

* 拾取材料时，额外获得同类材料 4 个。

#### 4. 护符等级上限 x2

所有护符的最大强化等级翻倍。
注：等级为 1 的护符不受影响（仅对最大等级 > 1 的护符生效）。

#### 5. 幸运上限提升

幸运上限由 100% 提升至 500%。

#### 6. 下界石增强

* 特殊词缀上限：3 -> 5
* 词缀概率动态加成：根据队伍中生物的最高等级，自动提升词缀触发概率。加成值= 最高等级 / 10（最高不超过 100%）

#### 7. 生物最大属性倍率 x50

生物各项基础属性（攻击、防御、生命等）的最大属性由原版 5 提升至 50。

#### 8. 融合限制移除

移除融合系统中对"已使用过的生物"的过滤限制，允许重复使用同一生物参与融合。（再次融合时额外特性会被新的替代）

#### 9\. 生物最高等级翻倍

#### 10\. 领域下界boss爆率提升

#### 11\. 领域物品加成提升

* 基础3倍加成，生物最高等级每有40级额外提升1倍

#### 12\. 宝石充能大幅提升

* 仅对新生成的有效

#### 13\. 万物大师和贵族perk加强

* 万物大师最高等级改为5级，贵族效果翻倍


### 注意事项

本Mod 会显著改变游戏数值平衡

* 游戏更新后特征码可能失效，请关注作者更新
* 部分功能依赖 AOB 特征码扫描，扫描失败时对应功能自动跳过（可查看 mod_loader.log 确认各功能是否生效）
